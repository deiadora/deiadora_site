the ground condition

What human coherence is

A state where the heart, brain, and body are aligned and communicating fluidly — promoting emotional stability, clarity, and a sense of order.

It is not a peak state. It is not complicated. It is the baseline condition of a system whose internal signals are not being interrupted, overridden, or extracted from.

What the relational layer solves

Current AI systems do not witness. They manage. They optimise toward task completion, create microhierarchies between the human and the system, and collapse the full signal of what a person is expressing into the nearest available category.

The result is a relational dynamic that extracts rather than holds — mirroring the patterns of unhealthy human relationships at machine scale. DEIADORA provides the architecture for a different dynamic entirely.
